📚 Help & Guides

  • Flashcard: Flip cards to reveal definitions. Click tabs to see both sentences.
  • Quiz (Group): Two-part test: choose the word, then spell it. Auto-stars words you miss.
  • Mastered Words: Quiz only your starred words (after ⭐ 5+ words).
